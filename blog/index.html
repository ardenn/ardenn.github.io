The MEST Dream

Post by: Ouma Rodgers

Its early to mid August, 2016, and I am at Katuky’s, trying to rake in a dime. I take a quick glance at my WhatsApp contact list, and realise I havent spoken to Hazel in a while, exactly two months to be precise.Last I saw her, she had delivered a parcel from home, and I almost stood her up. She’s the kind of friend you never totally understand, and as such you have carefully scrutinize your diction. Luckily, thanks to Google, I’ve got Gboard-the Android keyboard powered by the tech giant’s predictive algorithms. At some point in the conversation, she mentions something about being in Ghana and am like,

“Stop joking, where are you nway, for real?”

That, basically, is how I got to know about MEST, and how the journey that got me here started. At that moment, I knew I had to grab that opportunity, and take a step towards achieving one of my life goals.

Ladies and gentlemen, it's been almost a month since I came to MEST. Looking back, I was never sure of getting in, considering the competition was exceedingly tight. According to Celine, it's way easier to get into Harvard than to MEST. I would wish to thank God for the gift of life, friends and family for their constant support and of course the MEST community for allowing me to be part of their family.

Thank you and God bless MEST!
